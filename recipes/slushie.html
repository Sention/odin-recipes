Watermelon Cooler Slushy!

This watermelon slushy is just perfect for hot summer days!

Watermelon, ice, and a bit of lime juice are simply blended together in this quick treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 10 ice cubes
  • ⅓ cup fresh lime juice
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Place watermelon and ice into a blender
  2. Pour in lime juice, sugar, and salt.
  3. lend until smooth.
